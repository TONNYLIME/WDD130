White Water Rafting

White Water Rafting companies has been formed to create the water rafting activity
in Kenya. Our mission is to get good use of our available water resource to create job
for the youth and revenue. our motto is to Swim on,build the future.

History

White Water Rafting company was started by Tonny in the 2024.It was start because it was observed
that in Kenya We have nice water resource that could be used for tourist attraction
and create job vacancies for the growing youth in Kenya.
